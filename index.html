The Land and Sea of Serene Beauty

MABINI

Mabini, Batangas is a coastal haven where mountains kiss the sea and coral reefs bloom beneath crystal waters. From the diving paradise of Anilao to the peaceful trails of Mt. Gulugod-Baboy, it's a place where nature, heritage, and adventure come alive. Simple, soulful, unforgettable.

MABINI EXPERIENCE
MOUNTAIN PEAK
MT. GULUGOD-BABOY
Hike to the summit of Mt. Gulugod-Baboy for breathtaking 360-degree views of Batangas Bay, Maricaban Strait, and the nearby islands. A relatively easy trek suitable for beginners with a rewarding panoramic vista at the top.
MABINI EXPERIENCE
CULTURAL FESTIVAL
MABINI FIESTA
Experience the vibrant culture of Mabini during the annual town fiesta featuring traditional dances, local cuisine, and colorful street celebrations. Immerse yourself in authentic Filipino hospitality and festive traditions.
MABINI EXPERIENCE
SEASIDE RETREAT
SEPOC BEACH
Relax at Sepoc Beach with its pristine white sand and crystal-clear waters. Enjoy swimming, snorkeling, or simply basking in the sun. Perfect for family outings and romantic getaways with stunning sunset views.
MABINI EXPERIENCE
DIVING PARADISE
ANILAO
Discover Anilao, the birthplace of scuba diving in the Philippines. Famous for its vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life including rare nudibranchs, sea turtles, and colorful tropical fish. Perfect for both beginners and experienced divers.
