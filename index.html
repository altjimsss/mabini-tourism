The Land and Sea of Serene Beauty

MABINI

Mabini, Batangas is a coastal haven where mountains kiss the sea and coral reefs bloom beneath crystal waters. From the diving paradise of Anilao to the peaceful trails of Mt. Gulugod-Baboy, it's a place where nature, heritage, and adventure come alive. Simple, soulful, unforgettable.

Discover Paradise

Immerse yourself in the natural wonders and rich culture of Mabini, where every moment creates lasting memories and every view takes your breath away.

Crystal Waters

Dive into pristine coral reefs and swim alongside vibrant marine life in some of the clearest waters in the Philippines.

Scenic Trails

Hike through lush mountains and discover breathtaking viewpoints that showcase the natural beauty of Batangas.

Local Cuisine

Savor authentic Filipino flavors with fresh seafood and traditional dishes that celebrate the region's culinary heritage.
